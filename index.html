Michael CeraVe

L'Oréal | 2024 Super Bowl

Campaign Overview

"Michael CeraVe" was a first-of-its-kind, bold, immersive campaign that invited the internet into a prank-like experience, only to be debunked on America's biggest stage – the Super Bowl. The three-week campaign took people on a journey from "CeraVe, developed by Michael Cera" to "CeraVe is developed with dermatologists." The integrated campaign was executed across earned, paid and owned channels, including 60+ influencers driving a viral phenomenon before the spot even aired.

Influencer Strategy & Execution

As influencer support on this campaign, I helped execute a comprehensive social and influencer conspiracy theory that built anticipation and virality. The strategy leveraged cultural conversation and meme culture, inclusive of innovatively locking and co-creating content with the largest global meme accounts within 48 hours to achieve organic virality.

Recognition & Awards

Named #1 Super Bowl Commercial by Forbes, AdWeek, and AdAge. The campaign won the 2024 Cannes Grand Prix, received an Emmy Nomination for Best Ad, and became the 3rd most awarded campaign in the world. The collaboration with @goodnewsdog achieved the account's highest performing content ever with 14M impressions and 760K shares in 24 hours.

Heads of CeraVe 🫧

L'Oréal | 2025

Campaign Overview

Heads of CeraVe is a three-phased category launch that continued to push the boundaries of influencer-led marketing for CeraVe and L'Oréal. Building on the viral momentum of Michael CeraVe, this campaign was designed to sustain cultural relevance and drive continued brand affinity through strategic creator partnerships and earned media, with continuous analysis of audience, culture, competitors, and brand reputation to sharpen strategic positioning and fuel agile storytelling.

Influencer Strategy & Execution

As a strategist, I developed the creator brief, managed talent relationships, and oversaw content amplification to ensure cohesive messaging while preserving authentic creator voices. The approach was rooted in earned-first, go-to-market thinking — drawing on social listening insights to shape integrated creative briefs and deploy edutaining, cross-platform (Meta, YouTube, TikTok, X) celebrity partnerships.

CeraVe GOATed is a cultural moment campaign that tapped into internet culture and sports fandom to position CeraVe as a top-tier, GOAT-level skincare brand. The campaign generated massive organic conversation, fueled by strategic influencer partnerships and a culturally resonant creative concept.

Coca-Cola Move

Rosalía Co-Creation | 2023

Campaign Overview

Coca-Cola Move was a global campaign launching the limited-edition Coca-Cola x Rosalía collaboration. The campaign fused music, fashion, and beverage culture to create a multi-platform cultural moment — driving massive earned media and consumer engagement through a strategic blend of celebrity partnership and creator amplification.

Influencer Strategy & Execution

I developed and executed the influencer strategy, identifying creators across music, lifestyle, and fashion verticals to extend the reach of the Rosalía partnership. The campaign deployed tiered influencer activations that I managed from start to finish — from VIP seeding and exclusive event access to organic creator content — generating authentic enthusiasm across key markets globally. I also personally hosted 85 international influencers, leading them through a day's worth of planned activities and content creation in NYC.

#Totalmente

Verizon | 2024 Super Bowl

Campaign Overview

Verizon's Total Wireless #Totalmente was a Super Bowl campaign celebrating authentic Hispanic culture. With only 5 days to execute, the influencer strategy was built from the ground up — identifying, briefing, and activating a diverse roster of Spanish-speaking creators whose content authentically aligned with their audience interests and creative verticals, bolstering cultural excitement around the brand's biggest moment of the year.

Influencer Strategy & Execution

I ensured that co-created content was authentically aligned with audience interests and creator verticals, driving genuine cultural excitement. Coca-Cola Soccer

FIFA World Cup, MLS | 2022, 2023

Campaign Overview

Throughout my work on Coca-Cola, I executed a variety of activations driving brand affinity within soccer culture - from event planning and hosting, to content co-creation, to influencer management.

FIFA World Cup: To kick off the FIFA World Cup 2022, we helped Coca-Cola celebrate the passionate journey of football fans by activating influencers across the global FIFA World Cup Trophy Tour and "Believing is Magic" campaign to generate brand awareness and drive attendance to local Trophy Tour events.

MLS: In 2023, we partnered with Coca-Cola to drive attendance, awareness, and social buzz around the Beats, Eats & Cleats MLS event by hosting diverse influencers that represented different soccer passion verticals (fandom, gaming, pro freestyling).

Influencer Strategy & Execution

FIFA World Cup: We partnered with a carefully curated mix of 13 micro to mega influencers — including two-time FIFA Women's World Cup champion Carli Lloyd, FIFA E-Sports streamer Edwin Castro, and Gen Z TikTok stars — to authentically connect Coca-Cola's campaign to passionate football communities.

MLS: We activated 3 micro to macro influencers spanning the soccer fandom, eMLS gaming, and freestyling verticals, leveraging longstanding Coca-Cola creator partnerships to ensure authentic content, brand loyalty, and strong community reach across Instagram and TikTok. I supported in event planning of Beats, Cleats, and Eats, overseeing influencers and content creation throughout the weekend festivities.
